Bifrost workspaces can be packaged as portable bundles and applied to a different environment. This is the supported way to move forms, agents, workflows, and apps between dev, staging, and production, or to share a configuration with the community.

The `bifrost export --portable` command writes a scrubbed bundle (no org IDs, no role IDs, no per-environment secrets) to a directory of your choice. `bifrost import` then applies that bundle to a target environment, rewriting org and role references to match the destination.
